ECSE Research Exhibition Day (RED) 2025

The Electrical, Computer, and Software Engineering (ECSE) Research Exhibition Day (RED) is an annual event that showcases the innovative research and projects undertaken by students and faculty in the School of Engineering and Design. This year's event promises to be an exciting opportunity for students to present their work, network with industry professionals, and gain valuable feedback on their research.

Participants will have the chance to engage with a diverse range of projects, from cutting-edge engineering solutions to sustainable technologies. The event will feature keynote speakers from industry leaders, interactive workshops, and panel discussions on the latest trends in engineering research.

Whether you are a student looking to showcase your work, a faculty member seeking to connect with industry experts, or simply interested in learning more about the innovative research happening at ECSE, the RED is an event not to be missed.

Join us for a day of inspiration, collaboration, and discovery as we celebrate the achievements of our engineering community. We look forward to seeing you at the ECSE Research Exhibition Day!
